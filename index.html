About Me

Hi! I'm John, I am a Full-Stack Web Developer based in Los Angeles California. My admiration for coding began when I was twelve years old. I was introduced to MySpace and was addicted to the customization aspect that it provided. I would spend countless hours editing the html, and would always show off my profile to all my peers.

After graduating from the University of California Riverside, i decided to venture into the medical field and worked as a lab assistant. I would constantly run into problems with my company's website being down or under construction, and always wondered how i could fix their website myself.

My curiousity led me to enrolling at Learningfuze Coding BootCamp, where i spent over 700 hours learning new languages and the best coding practices. Since graduating, coding has become my new lifestyle, if im not physically coding, im always thinking about ways to refactor my code or how to debug a problem for a project im working on. I'm always looking to connect and broaden my network, feel free to hit me up!
